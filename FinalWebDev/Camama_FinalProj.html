About Me

I am Earl Justin Paalan Camama, born in Puerto Princesa City, Palawan. I was already exposed to computers at a very young age, and I took an interest in tinkering computers. Being around computers during my childhood, I wanted to pursue a field within it, that is why I studied Information Technology. During my childhood, I found it hard to socialize with others. Still, during my Senior High School, I was more exposed to people and problems encountered by people, and this ignited the fire within me.

I started joining organizations that have the same advocacy as mine, I volunteered for change, and I took an interest in doing things that I knew would bring change to our world.
